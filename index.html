Delicious Momo Dumplings

Ingredients

  • 500g minced meat (chicken, pork, or beef)
  • 1 cup finely chopped cabbage
  • 1/2 cup grated carrots
  • 2 finely chopped onions
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, finely sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 pack momo wrappers or homemade dough

Method

  1. In a large bowl, combine the minced meat, cabbage, carrots, onions, garlic, ginger, and green chilies.
  2. Add soy sauce, salt, and black pepper to the mixture. Mix thoroughly.
  3. Take a momo wrapper and place a spoonful of the filling in the center.
  4. Moisten the edges of the wrapper with water and fold to seal the dumpling, leaving a small opening at the top.
  5. Arrange the momos on a steamer lined with parchment paper.
  6. Steam over boiling water for 15-20 minutes, or until the dough is cooked and the filling is tender.
  7. Serve hot with a side of spicy tomato chutney or soy sauce.

About Momo

Momo is a popular street food in Nepal, Tibet, and regions of India. This recipe offers an authentic taste with a twist of your choice of meat or vegetables, making it perfect as a snack or a meal on its own!
